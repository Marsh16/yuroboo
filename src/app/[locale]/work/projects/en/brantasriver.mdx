## Overview

Brantas River is an endless runner mobile game that took place in the brantas river, the longest river in East Java Province.
In the game, the player took role of a boat who travels down the river while avoiding rocks that scattered along the way. As they travel, they grab as many trash as they can.
The player needs to tilt their phone to the left or right to control the boat's movement. The boar will sink if it hit the rock at which point the game ends.

## Key Features

- **Tilt to Play**: Have motion gesture features, to move the player and collect items tilt your phone
- **Collect Trash and Avoid Rocks**: Can collect trash and die when hitting rocks

## Technologies Used

- **Motion Sensors**: Using CoreMotion for motion sensors in game
- **3D Object**: Using SceneKit to create 3D environment and blender to create 3D Objects. 

## Tech Stack

- **Apple Techs**: GameplayKit, Core Motion, SceneKit, SwiftUI, Core Haptics, Audio, Blender 

## Challenges and Learnings

A fun iOS game for people to play and collect trash in the brantas river using apple technology such as GameplayKit, Core Motion, SceneKit, SwiftUI

## Outcome

SceneKit, Core Motion and integrating GameplayKit in the 3d game for the ECS Architecture, Audio in game, and Core Haptics
