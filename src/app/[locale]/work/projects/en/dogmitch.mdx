## Overview

DogMitch is a dedicated app for finding reliable dog breeding partners. It ensures safety and trust by requiring detailed health verification, including pedigree, vaccine records, and medical history. This rigorous process guarantees that every match is based on accurate, verified data, providing dog owners with confidence in their breeding choices. DogMitch creates a secure platform for connecting with suitable mates, promoting responsible and healthy breeding practices.

## Key Features

- **Find Mate**: Search for suitable mate for your dog online. 
- **Connect**: After you found the perfect match. Contact the owner and meet up. 

## Technologies Used

- **Geo Location (Maps, Beacons)**: Location enabled for easier input
- **3rd Party Framework Integration**: Using Firebase as the online database and image storage.   

## Tech Stack

- **Apple Techs**: SwiftUI, Maps
- **Third Party**: Firebase

## Challenges and Learnings

Finding a mate for your dog requires considering multiple aspects, such as health and breed. This simplifies it: a Tinder for Dogs.

## Outcome

1. Responsible Breeding: Breeding dogs should be done responsibly with careful consideration of the health and well-being of both parents and potential offspring.
2. Ethical Breeding Practices: Ethical breeders prioritize the health, temperament, and welfare of their dogs. They often conduct health tests and genetic screenings to minimize the risk of passing on genetic diseases.
