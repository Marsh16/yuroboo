## Overview

ArchCalculator is an app to help Architecture Student Calculate Structural Plans. There are many categories, such as Column, Foundation, Blocks, Scale and Roof. Each categories have their own calculation which calculate according to the type of structure of each category.

## How it Works?

1. Choose the category (Column, Foundation, Blocks, Scale or Roof)

2. Input the value you want to calculate

3. Press the calculate button or recalculate button (if you want to recalculate)

4. Read your result!

## Technologies Used

- **Calculate**: Calculate your category of structure faster.
- **Search**: Search your structure easily.
- **Notification**: Notification when calculation completed.

## Tech Stack

- **Apple Techs**: SwiftUI, Notifications

## Challenges and Learnings

Calculating so many structural plan is hard for architecture students, with this app student can just choose and calculate faster without searching for too long on the internet. Dont't waste your time!

## Outcome

ArchCalculator made calculating structural plan easier and faster
